🌱 About PlantAI

PlantAI is an AI-powered plant disease detection platform designed to help farmers, gardeners, researchers, and agricultural experts identify plant diseases quickly and accurately through leaf image analysis.

By leveraging deep learning and computer vision technologies, PlantAI enables early disease detection, helping reduce crop losses, improve productivity, and support smarter agricultural decisions.

🌐 Project Overview

PlantAI provides a simple workflow:

  • Upload a plant leaf image
  • AI analyzes the image
  • Disease is identified
  • Results and recommendations are displayed instantly

📊 Dataset Information

Original Dataset

  • Total Images: 87,000+ RGB Images
  • Classes: 38 Plant Disease Categories
  • Resolution: 256 × 256 Pixels
  • Coverage: Multiple Crops and Diseases

Training Configuration

  • Training Images: 70,295 (80%)
  • Validation Images: 17,572 (20%)
  • Test Images: 33 Real-World Samples
  • Augmentation: Rotation, Flipping and Zoom

    • Supported Crops & Disease Classes

    The AI model has been trained on 38 plant health categories covering multiple crops, diseases, and healthy plant conditions.

    • Apple: Apple Scab, Black Rot, Cedar Apple Rust, Healthy
    • Blueberry: Healthy
    • Cherry: Powdery Mildew, Healthy
    • Corn (Maize): Cercospora Leaf Spot, Common Rust, Northern Leaf Blight, Healthy
    • Grape: Black Rot, Esca (Black Measles), Leaf Blight, Healthy
    • Orange: Huanglongbing (Citrus Greening)
    • Peach: Bacterial Spot, Healthy
    • Bell Pepper: Bacterial Spot, Healthy
    • Potato: Early Blight, Late Blight, Healthy
    • Raspberry: Healthy
    • Soybean: Healthy
    • Squash: Powdery Mildew
    • Strawberry: Leaf Scorch, Healthy
    • Tomato: Bacterial Spot, Early Blight, Late Blight, Leaf Mold, Septoria Leaf Spot, Spider Mites, Target Spot, Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus, Tomato Mosaic Virus, Healthy
